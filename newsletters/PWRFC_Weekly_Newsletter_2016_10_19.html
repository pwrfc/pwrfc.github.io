October 19, 2016
Captain's Log: Philadelphia vs Harrisburg
     This past weekend Philly played our last D2 crossover match against Harrisburg.  Our opponent requested we play 3 - 30 minute periods in order to rotate the maximum number of players in, which we were happy to accommodate.  We have had the benefit of a deep bench this year with talented players and tough competition for positions.  This gave us the chance to try some new combinations, give players time to grow into new positions and work on building the strongest roster for our game next week against NOVA.
     Despite forces beyond our control that severely disrupted the flow of the game, Philly was able to dominate the scrums, implement new forward patterns and execute dynamic back-line plays.  Our back-line defense was able to come up with speed and shut down Harrisburg's forward momentum at the centers.  Once again our flankers and 8 men were the work horses of the game with tackle after tackle, hard hitting strike lines and producing turnover ball.  Becca "Loose Balls" Fagan earned the honor of B*tch of the Pitch this week, punishing Harrisburg for dropped balls by being all over it like white on rice and schooling the field on the art of ball placement.  Her support on defense would have also earned her the record for poached balls had we had the opportunity to exercise our right to the ball.  Earning her first yellow card for Philly and Silent Force is Kate "Midge" Cardone.  Her direction at scrumhalf was critical in executing our new forward Dry plays and her ability to take down players four times her size was impressive to everyone but the sir.  For the first time this season, an outside source caused tempers to flare and Philly to become a little frazzled, but somewhere between being called "offsides" while running a straight forward punch on offense and being penalized for having our hands up in the air on a line out, we were able to pull it together as a team and finish the game strong.  More important than the win last Saturday is the knowledge that when things get out of hand, we are able to reset and refocus and come together to get the job done.  
Kate Hallinan
Captain
This Saturday, Philly kicks off at 1pm against NOVA at Edgely Field. This is a league match. We would love to have the support of our friends, family, and alumnae to cheer us on!
Captain's Note
In order to provide more opportunities for beginner players and provide an outlet for players who may be unable to commit to being a Divison 1 player, Philly is planning on forming a D3 side for the Fall 2017 season. We look forward to hearing feedback from alum and would love for anyone interested in being involved to reach out!
October is Breast Cancer Awareness Month
This Saturday, Philly Women will recognize Breast Cancer Awareness Month by wearing pick socks when we take the field with NOVA. Profits from the player sock sales will be donated to the American Cancer Society. We encourage our fans and spectators to wear pink and considering making a donation as well.  Let's #ruckcancer together!
