Player Responsibilities

Dues

All players are expected to pay dues. However, we never want a player's financial situation to keep them from playing rugby with us. Our treasurer can accomodate payment plans for help with dues and CIPP fees. Before their first game, all players are expected to have CIPPed themselves and at least set up a payment plan (if not paying full dues at once). For any questions about dues, payment plans, or CIPPing, email our treasurer at treasurer@pwrfc.org.

Linc Shifts and the "Points System"

In order to run the club and keep the cost of dues low for players, our club organizes many fundraising efforts. Our biggest source of income is working a food stand for Aramark at Lincoln Financial Field for Eagles Games. Our Linc coordinator calculates the minimum number of shifts each player is required to work in order for us to keep our contract with Aramark. For the 2016 season, returning players are required to work 3 shifts and rookies 2 shifts. The penalty for a playing skipping their Linc shift without finding a replacement is $60/shift.

The "points system" is the method by which the club ensures that players are equally contributing to club administrative and fundraising tasks. At the conclusion of each spring season, players who have earned a surplus of points above the average are paid for their extra effort in helping the club run. Players who have accumulated points below average will pay penalties. Points can be earned in a number of ways including: Working Linc shifts, organize a fundraising, hold a board or chair position, wash team jerseys or pinnies, or even invite friends to our fundraisers.
